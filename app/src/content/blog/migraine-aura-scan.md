The MigraineAuraScan turns lived symptoms into a structured, shareable report. By guiding patients through a careful anamnesis — one question at a time — it helps surface the aura symptoms that are easy to miss in a short consultation, and frames them in the language clinicians use to diagnose.

This post introduces the tool, the thinking behind its three-section flow, and how a patient-derived report can shorten the path to recognition and care.
